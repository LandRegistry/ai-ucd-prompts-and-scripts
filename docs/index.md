## Introduction

Ai can help to assist designers in:

- speeding up design work
- improving the quality of work (at the design stage, or later when being implemented in teams, or reviewing after its been put into a test environment or shipped to users)
- doing design work we could not do before without help (e.g. didnt have the skills or time to do it or learn it)
- communicating

These prompts and scripts have been used for real purposes, but are work in progress - experiments being iterated. 

This site is a repository of scripts, not a how to guide to using them safely or responsiblly. You should use them responsibly and safely and do your own checking of the results. 

Most Government design work is rule based - we have proven, clear, tested, solutions and guidance for patterns, components and writing style. Rules also exist for accessibilty, and open data standards.  Much of this can be given as rules to Ai to follow and it has a large body of knowledge and 'rules' it has generated in these areas.
